🌊 Cambugahay Falls

A multi-tiered natural waterfall hidden within the green heart of Siquijor. Known for its turquoise waters, rope swings, and peaceful jungle atmosphere, it offers a refreshing escape for travelers seeking nature and adventure.

“Not just a place — but a feeling of calm hidden inside the island.”
Nature Attraction

📍 Hidden Path Location

Reached through scenic village roads and jungle trails in Siquijor.

🚶 Travel Experience

Feels like entering a hidden natural paradise surrounded by greenery.

🌿 Atmosphere

Cool, refreshing, and peaceful — perfect for relaxation and photos.

💡 Visitor Tip

Visit early morning for the best lighting and fewer tourists.
