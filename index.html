About Cambugahay Falls

Cambugahay Falls is one of the most famous tourist destinations located in Lazi, Siquijor, Philippines. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise waters and multi-level waterfalls, it attracts thousands of visitors every year.

The falls are surrounded by lush tropical vegetation that creates a peaceful and refreshing atmosphere. Its natural spring water remains cool and clean throughout the year.
