About Cambugahay Falls

A natural paradise hidden in Siquijor

🌿 The Beauty of Nature

Cambugahay Falls is one of the most iconic natural attractions in Siquijor. Known for its crystal-clear turquoise water, layered waterfalls, and peaceful jungle surroundings, it offers a refreshing escape from busy city life.

🏊

Activities

Swimming, bamboo rafting, and rope swinging for adventure lovers.

📸

Photography

Perfect destination for travel content, nature shots, and aesthetic visuals.

✨

Experience

Relaxing waterfall sounds and cool waters give a peaceful natural escape.
