Find Cambugahay Falls

Hidden paradise in Siquijor, Philippines

📍 Lazi, Siquijor, Philippines
✨ Famous natural 3-tier waterfall paradise

📍 Exact Location

Cambugahay Falls is located in Lazi, Siquijor Island, Philippines. One of the most visited natural attractions in the province.

Open for Tourists

🚗 How to Get There

From Siquijor Port, ride a tricycle or habal-habal going to Lazi town. Travel time is around 30–45 minutes.

💡 Travel Tip

Visit early in the morning for fewer crowds and crystal-clear turquoise water perfect for swimming, photography, and rope swinging.
